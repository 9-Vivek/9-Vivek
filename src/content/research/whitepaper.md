The ACC Technical Whitepaper will consolidate the framework's theoretical grounding and connect it to implementable software architecture.

## Planned scope

The paper will define a portable model for active context, describe the boundary between inferred and user-authored state, and propose evaluation criteria for continuity, legibility, and agency.

It will also document lessons from the first reference implementations, including the costs of semantic indexing, the role of local inference, and failure modes created by stale or overconfident context models.
