The AI Systems Institute is an early concept for applied research, technical education, and project-based study around modern AI systems.

## Purpose

Most AI education focuses either on model theory or application-level prompting. The institute would focus on the systems between those layers: context assembly, evaluation, tool use, local inference, data boundaries, observability, and human control.

## Program direction

- Publish practical systems notes and reference architectures.
- Develop project-based curricula around local and distributed AI systems.
- Provide a setting for applied experiments connected to ACC and PIL.
- Treat evaluation, privacy, and operational clarity as core engineering topics.

The initiative is not yet a formal organization. This page records its intended scope and relationship to the broader research program.
